About the Developer and the Vision

Hello, and welcome. I am Khan Hassan Riaz, a visually impaired software developer with a degree in Special Education. I built this platform to share the software and tools I develop, with a primary focus on making education and technology accessible for everyone.

When I started learning software development, my goal was simple: create solutions that break down barriers. I know firsthand the challenges students face when study materials are not fully accessible. ProQuiz Simulator was born out of this need. It is not just an educational app; it is a step toward ensuring that every candidate, whether sighted or visually impaired, has an equal opportunity to prepare for competitive exams.

What is ProQuiz Simulator?

ProQuiz Simulator is a complete digital testing academy. It is designed specifically for aspirants preparing for PPSC, FPSC, NTS, and other competitive exams. Instead of jumping between different books and websites, you get a one-stop solution right on your Windows PC or Android device.

Core Features That Set It Apart

Massive Learning Database

The platform comes with a built-in offline database of over 15,000 authentic General Knowledge MCQs, including subjects like Computer Science, Current Affairs, English, Everyday Science, General Knowledge, Geography, Islamic Studies, Mathematics, Pakistan Studies, and Urdu. This ensures you have enough material to practice continuously.

Comprehensive Special Education Coverage

For candidates in the Special Education field, we provide a complete and authentic syllabus with Roman Urdu translation based on 20 subjects, specifically designed for preparation for Senior and Junior Special Education Teacher exams.

  • Administration and management for special education
  • Assessment and evaluation
  • Assistive Technology
  • Braille
  • Classroom Management
  • Curriculum adaptation for children with special needs
  • Development and implementation of IEP
  • Disabilities
  • Educational psychology
  • Guidance and counseling
  • Human Growth and Development
  • Inclusive Education
  • Independent living skills
  • Orientation and mobility
  • Philosophy
  • Physical Education
  • Research process
  • Special education
  • Visual impairment

We recently expanded the platform to cover all major disabilities. It includes complete subjective guides and notes for the Visual Impairment field, along with brand-new sections for Hearing Impairment, Physical Disability, and Intellectual Disability. This makes it the ultimate tool for PPSC Special Education Teacher exams.

FPSC Lecturer Grade 17 Syllabus

We have integrated the complete syllabus for this specific exam in a very easy-to-understand format to help you master the required concepts.

SEER Foundation GK Course

Rote memorization only gets you so far. We included the subjective General Knowledge course from the SEER Foundation to help you truly understand the concepts behind the facts.

English Learning Course

Clearing the written exam is just half the battle. The built-in English module helps you refine your communication skills for the interview phase and your professional career.

Create Your Own Custom Quizzes

You are not limited to our database. You can copy text from your own notes, or upload your PDF and text files, and the software will instantly generate a custom test for you.

Real Exam Environment

Practice makes perfect, especially under pressure. You can generate full mock tests covering all subjects based on the exam pattern. The built-in timer ensures you learn how to manage your time effectively before the actual test day.

Smart Study Mode

Think of this as your personal tutor. The system tracks the questions you answer incorrectly and repeatedly tests you on them until you have memorized the correct answers.

Built for Accessibility

True to my core mission, ProQuiz Simulator is 100 percent accessible. It works flawlessly with screen readers on both Android and Windows. The interface is clean, easy to navigate, and fully inclusive.

Availability and Pricing

You can download the app from the Google Play Store or get the Windows setup directly from this site. Every user gets a full 24-hour free trial to test all features. Once you are satisfied, you can unlock the lifetime license for both platforms for just 1999 PKR.

Looking Ahead
